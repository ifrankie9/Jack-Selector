What can I do to reduce the size of the actuator result?

  1.   Reduce the Travel Rate (speed)

  2.   Reduce the Duty

  3.   For High Duty running, Switch to Ball Screw

  4.   For Long Column Length required, Guide the load

  5.   Re-Design for a Tension Load to eliminate the Column Strength
